Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 7, 2024

1. Introduction

Event Sync ("we", "our", or "us") provides a service that synchronizes local events from Luma to your Google Calendar. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

When you use Event Sync, we collect and store:

  • Email Address: Obtained through Google OAuth to identify your account
  • Google Calendar Access: Limited to calendars created by this app only
  • Sync Preferences: Event category (e.g., AI, Tech, Food) and geographic area (map coordinates)
  • OAuth Tokens: Refresh tokens to maintain calendar sync without repeated authorization

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Create and maintain a dedicated Google Calendar for synced events
  • Fetch events from Luma matching your selected category and location
  • Automatically update your calendar with new events on a regular schedule
  • Identify your account for updates to sync preferences

4. Data Storage

Your information is stored in:

  • Google Sheets: Email, refresh token, sync preferences, and calendar ID
  • Your Google Calendar: Event data synced from Luma

We do not use external databases or third-party storage services.

5. Google API Services User Data Policy

Event Sync's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Specifically:

  • We only request the minimum necessary permissions (calendar.app.created scope)
  • We do not share your Google user data with third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising or marketing purposes
  • We do not allow humans to read your data unless required for security, compliance, or with your explicit permission

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Google: To perform calendar synchronization via Google Calendar API
  • Luma: To fetch public event data (no personal information is shared)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

7. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as you use the service. Your data remains in our database until:

  • You manually delete the calendar created by Event Sync
  • You revoke Event Sync's access in your Google Account permissions
  • You request deletion by contacting us (see Contact section)

8. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • OAuth tokens are stored securely and never exposed to the client
  • Communication with Google APIs uses HTTPS encryption
  • Limited calendar scope prevents access to your existing calendars
  • Open-source code available for audit at GitHub

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View what data we store about you (stored in Google Sheets, contact us for access)
  • Deletion: Delete your data by revoking access or contacting us
  • Modification: Update your sync preferences anytime by reauthorizing with new settings
  • Revocation: Revoke access at Google Account permissions

10. Children's Privacy

Event Sync is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

12. Contact Us

For questions, data requests, or to delete your data, please:

  • Open an issue on GitHub
  • Email: ruidiao@googlegroups.com
